Meeting Schedule

The George H. W. Bush Composite Squadron meets every Tuesday from 6:30pm (cadets) or 7:00pm (seniors) until 9:00pm.  Special training events are frequently scheduled on weekends.  Our Tuesday meeting locations vary as shown below.

Meeting Locations

First – Third Tuesdays (and Fifth Tuesdays unless otherwise noted): Portable Building in FBO Parking Lot of Coulter Airfield in Bryan, Texas.
