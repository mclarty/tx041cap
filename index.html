In Memoriam

The members of the George H. W. Bush Composite Squadron express our deepest condolences to the surviving family of President George H. W. Bush. As with other Americans and those around the world, we mourn the loss and celebrate the extraordinary life of President Bush and his devoted commitment to public service.

President Bush stood as a role model for all those who follow him in service to our country. His memory will forever live in the hearts and minds of our squadron's members, and as his namesake we shall relentlessly carry on his legacy of public service as we serve our community, state and nation.
